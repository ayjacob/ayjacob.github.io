Instructions

A sudoku is logic puzzle is made up of 3 boxes by 3 boxes and in each box there are 3 squares by 3 squares (making a total of 81 squares). The goal is to fill in each each row, column, and box with the numbers 1-9. Good Luck!

License Plate Game:

Instructions

The License plate game is a game that you can play by yourself or with another person or with many other peple. It is Ideal for a long road trip.

To play, you simply look out the window of your car and see how many different license plates you can see. Whenever you see one you haven't seen yet, you mark it down. If you are playing with other people then whoever has the most at the end wins.

At the Begining it will be easy but as it goes on it may not be so often that you see a license plate you haven't seen yet so keep your eyes peeled!

Would You Rather:

Instructions

Would you rather is an easy game to play. By each set, there are 2 choices. Think about which one you think is better for a few minutes, then click next to it and move onto the next one.

If you enjoyed them when you finished, ask a friend or family member to to play with you. You can take turns coming up with one, or one of you can come up with all of them. You can also with play with more than one other person. Enjoy!
